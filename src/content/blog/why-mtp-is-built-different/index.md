Most software companies sell you a tool. Maybe it is a great tool. Maybe it does exactly what it says on the tin.

But here is the problem: your business does not run on one tool. It runs on ten. And getting those ten tools to talk to each other is where most of the pain lives.

## The Integration Tax

Every time you add a new tool to your stack, you pay an integration tax. You spend time setting up connections, dealing with API limits, debugging webhooks and maintaining fragile automations built on top of tools that were never designed to work together.

Over time, this integration tax becomes enormous. Teams spend entire working days every week just keeping their software stack running, rather than doing actual work.

## What My Tech Passport Does Differently

My Tech Passport is not another tool to add to your stack. It is the layer that sits across all your tools and connects them intelligently.

When you connect a tool to My Tech Passport, it does not just make the connection. It makes the tool available to every workflow in your account. One connection, unlimited use.

### Built-In Tools, Ready to Go

My Tech Passport also comes with a library of built-in tools: an SEO audit tool, invoice generator, AI transcription, whiteboard, QR code generator and more. These tools are already connected to the platform, so you can use them in workflows the moment you add them.

### One-Click Workflow Activation

Instead of spending days building custom automations, My Tech Passport gives you workflow templates that are ready to activate. Pick a template, connect your tools, and the workflow runs automatically.

## The Result

Businesses using My Tech Passport spend less time on software maintenance and more time on growth. That is the only metric that matters.
